Combined Systems

Enterprises almost never implement a single pattern in isolation. These are end-to-end systems that bring multiple capabilities together the way they are actually deployed — each with its use case, the problem solved, the key decision, and the proof points.

Production

Secure Enterprise Application Platform

A paved road from commit to production to monitoring that any team can deploy onto. A secure-by-default landing zone with a single governed ingress (edge WAF/CDN to load balancer), serverless containers and event-driven functions, a DevSecOps pipeline that builds, scans, signs, and deploys, central egress inspection, and an observability platform watching everything — with guardrails enforced across the board.

Combines: landing zone · secure public access · managed containers · serverless · DevSecOps · observability

Enterprise Resilience & Business Continuity

How the whole environment survives failure — a tested, RTO/RPO-driven continuity capability rather than best-effort. Active/passive multi-region with cross-region replication (sync or async per tier), health-based global traffic management, automated cutover and failback, rehearsed runbooks and game-days, and an architecture that can extend cross-cloud for provider-failure resilience.

Cloud Migration & Modernization

Approach: landing zone first · 6Rs disposition · dependency-aware waves · reversible at each step
